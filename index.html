Share your work. Be successful.

Open scholarship is good for the public and for you.

Increase your visibility

Build a name for yourself. Share your work and make it more visibile.

Reduce publishing costs

Open publishing can cost the same or less than traditional publishing.

Take back control

Know your rights. Keep your rights. Decide how your work is used.

Get more funding

Meet funder requirements, and qualify for special funds.

Publish where you want

Publish in the journal of your choice and archive an open copy.

Get that promotion

Open research is increasingly recognized in promotion and tenure.
